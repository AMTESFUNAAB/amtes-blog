About The Competition

This is Nigeria's premier Mechatronics inter-varsity competition, challenging undergraduates to conceptualize and build real-world solutions. We aim to promote innovation and problem-solving among engineering students.

The Goal: Promote innovation and creativity and connect industries to top Mechatronics talents in Nigeria.

Focus Areas:

  • Artificial Intelligence (AI)
  • Internet of Things (IoT)
  • Industry 5.0 Technologies
  • Robotics & Automation
  • Software Solutions

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Who is eligible to apply? ▼

A: Undergraduates from partner universities offering Mechatronics Engineering.

A: Projects involving robotics, automation, AI, IoT, and software solutions.

Q: How many projects make it to the live finale? ▼

A: The top 7 projects will be evaluated based on quality and engagement to compete live.
